PromptPilot Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

What we collect

PromptPilot does not collect, store, or log any personally identifiable information. Your prompts are sent to our server solely to generate an improved version and are immediately discarded after processing. We do not store prompt content.

Local storage

We store only your daily usage count (a number) locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This never leaves your device.

Third parties

Prompt improvement is powered by Groq API. Prompts are processed and not retained. We do not sell or share any data with third parties.
